Tjap Tjoy

Tjap tjoy is usually made with cucumber, carrot, pepper, mushrooms and bean sprouts. These vegetables are fried in a salty, mild sauce. For meat lovers, pieces of chicken, pork or beef are also added

Gon bao kai

Gon bao kai is a good choice to eat. This spicy meal has a delicious sweet and sour taste. Our sauce tastes perfect with the vegetables and chicken in this dish

Tjauw min

Tjauw min is made from thin wheat noodles and is stir-fried with different types of vegetables. It is often prepared with meat, such as roasted chicken or Chinese sausage.

Pangsit goreng

Pangsit goreng is a tasty dish to eat. It is excellent as a snack or side dish. This fried dough snack is crispy and is served with a tasty dipping sauce.
